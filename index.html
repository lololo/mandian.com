Coins

Bitcoin value tracker

Coins makes it easy to see the value of your bitcoins. The exchange rate from bitcoin to 154 currencies is updated automatically every minute so you always know how much your bitcoins are worth.

Coins is a universal app and works on iPhone, iPod touch, iPad, and Android.

Simply the value of your wallet, at a glance.

Exchange rates provided by Coinbase.
